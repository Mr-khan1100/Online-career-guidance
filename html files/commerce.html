  • Bachelor of Management Studies(BMS)
  • Bachelor of Business Studies(BBS)
  • Bachelor in Business Administration(BBA)
  • Master of Commerce(M.Com)

    2. Operations

  • Company secretary Program(CS)

    • Admission Process:

    Most of the colleges offer admissions on the basis of merit for B.com or BBA courses after 12th commerce.Some colleges or universities provide admissions through national, state, university level entrance exams.Here, some entrance exams are enlisted for BBA aspirants:

    • AIMA UGAT (AIMA Under Graduate Aptitude Test)
    • SET (Symbiosis Entrance Test)
    • IPU CET (Indraprastha University Common Entrance Test)
    • CLAT (Common Law Admission Test)
    • LSAT (Law School Admission Test)
